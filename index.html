So, who is this guy?

I'm Mauro Consolani, I was born in Argentina, 26 six years ago. I was raised in Carlos Casares town and moved into La Plata city at the age of 19 to get my BA in Human Resources. Before that, I spent one year in Susanville, California as an exchange student sponsored by Rotary Club.

In the last three years I've worked mainly as HR Consultant, but since last year I've shfited to the awesome world of technologies. Now, I'm a self-taught web developer. Started playing around with Python, then HTML and CSS, followed by Bootstrap and currently building my path into Javascript.

So you may wonder... How I got here? Two main reasons: the people in my life, and convictions. What am I convinced about exactly?

That I am going to become a valuable developer! One Happy Developer. How? With a team.

Pasto a Pasto

Pasto a Pasto is meant to measure the forage offer level for stockbreeding establishments to optimize their productivity. Responsive design; backend with Flask; deployment on Heroku.
This app was developed together with three awesome team mates. Git flow, Jira, team organization, team communication and logical creativity were the foundation stones for this project.
